About Us

Heartfelt Facility will be a standard and licensed facility that will be located in San Diego, California.
Heartfelt will aim at providing a temporary home to homeless people, teenagers who escape home, homeless veterans, homeless single mothers, victims of domestic violence and any other person without somewhere to call home.
It will be a neatly renovated and secured spacious housing facility.
The facility will be specifically designed and equipped with the needed accommodations to give comfort and security to all its residents irrespective of religious affiliations, race, and health condition.
Heartfelt will be health-conscious and customer-centric, with a service culture deeply rooted in the fabric of our organizational structure.
With that, we will be able to consistently achieve our set business goals, increase our profitability and reinforce our positive long-term relationships with our clientele.
Our accommodation facility will be decorated with an exquisite facade, so much so that it will be a conspicuous tower in the city where it is located.
Heartfelt will provide a conducive home for our residents; we will engage in services that will help residents in our facility fully integrate into society.
The Heartfelt facility will be equipped with everything to make life comfortable for all our residents.
We will build a praying room, fitness room, and library and install free Wi-Fi to enable our residents and guests to surf the internet in their rooms free of charge.
There will be wireless access in all the public areas within the facility.

Mission

To eradicate homelessness and ensure that everyone including homeless single mothers, victims of domestic violence, immediate evictions visitors, retired veterans, young girls and boys, among others have a place to call home by offering a safe, clean, conducive and inviting environment.

Vision

To meet and surpass the needs of all homeless people in California

Slogan

Less people on the road, more shelter on the street keeps homelessness away. Make the world a better place; lets build a home for the homeless!
